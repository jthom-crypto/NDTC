# NDTC Application Portfolio

Three projects built for my application to the Institutional Sales Associate role at National Digital Trust Company (In Organization).

**Live site:** https://jthom-crypto.github.io/NDTC/

## Projects

**01. Institutional Prospect Pipeline**
A segmented target map across banks, credit unions, broker-dealers, corporate treasuries, and fintechs, with 24 named institutions, sizing, signal, and sourcing for each.
→ [`prospect-pipeline.html`](./prospect-pipeline.html)

**02. RBFCU Opportunity Assessment**
An internal-style prospect briefing built around Randolph-Brooks Federal Credit Union, showing how a custody opportunity would be researched, framed, and pitched internally.
→ [`rbfcu-opportunity-assessment.pdf`](./rbfcu-opportunity-assessment.pdf)

**03. Institutional Custody Competitive Battlecard**
A sales reference guide comparing NDTC to Anchorage, Coinbase, BitGo, Fidelity, Paxos, Circle, Ripple, and Fireblocks, with objection handling and sourced claims throughout.
→ [`battlecard.html`](./battlecard.html)

## Note

These are illustrative projects built for this application, based entirely on public information. They are not NDTC work product and do not reflect confirmed NDTC sales strategy, priorities, or client relationships.
